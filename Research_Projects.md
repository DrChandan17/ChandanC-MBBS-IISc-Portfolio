# Research Projects

## Early Motor Markers of Dementia
Ongoing work focusing on identifying subtle motor changes preceding cognitive decline and their potential role as early predictors of neurodegeneration.

## Sleep Deprivation and Cognitive Function
Study evaluating the effect of acute sleep deprivation on cognition and reaction time among medical interns.

## Acute Behavioral Disturbance
Clinical observations exploring neurological and cognitive correlates of acute behavioral disturbances.

## Digital Health – AI-based Refractive Error Detection
Developed a concept for an AI-supported smartphone-based tool to detect refractive error and suggest lens correction (JIPMER–IITB Incubate Hackathon).
